A unified platform for citizens to interact with Government

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can citizens participate in government decisions through Nagorik?

Citizens can engage in surveys and polls to express their opinions, allowing the government to align policies with public sentiment.

2. What can I do in the complaint section?

The complaint section lets you submit grievances, track their progress, and view government responsiveness to reported issues.

3. Can I see how the government responds to complaints?

Yes, public access to complaint statistics allows users to assess government responsiveness and identify areas needing improvement.
